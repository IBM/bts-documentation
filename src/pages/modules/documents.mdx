<PageDescription>

The **IBM Blockchain Transparent Supply™** **Documents** module enables **organizations** to upload,
view and share documents. The <a href={process.env.GATSBY_UI_URL + '/ift/api/documents/api-docs/'} target="_blank">
**Documents API**</a> is also available for developers to use REST API calls to manage documents.

</PageDescription>

## How it works

The **Documents** module enables your **organization** to upload, view and share
all types of documents, such as facility certificates and audit reports. You can
authorize specific organizations to view specific documents, and revoke the
privileges when necessary.
