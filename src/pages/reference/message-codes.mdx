<PageDescription>

**IBM Blockchain Transparent Supply™** message codes provide a standardized method for identifying and
understanding messages that are exchanged across the network. IBM Blockchain Transparent Supply™ supports
both GS1 Message Codes and the additional IBM Blockchain Transparent Supply™-specific message codes described below.

</PageDescription>

## Party role codes

**Party role codes** describe the types of participants that transact on IBM Blockchain Transparent Supply™.
For example, Accepting Party, Buyer, Carrier, and Despatch Party are message codes that
describe some of the participants on product supply chains.

Both **GS1 Party Role Codes** and **IBM Blockchain Transparent Supply™ Party role codes** can be used
in IBM Blockchain Transparent Supply™ messages. For the supported GS1 codes and descriptions, refer to
the <a href="http://apps.gs1.org/GDD/Pages/clDetails.aspx?semanticURN=urn:gs1:gdd:cl:PartyRoleCode&release=3"
target="_blank">GS1 Party Role Codes</a> (version 3&ndash;August 08, 2016). IBM Blockchain Transparent Supply™-specific
Party role codes, which can be used in addition to the GS1 Party Role Codes, are described in Table 1 below:

Table 1. IBM Blockchain Transparent Supply™ Party role codes

| IBM Blockchain Transparent Supply™ Party role code | Name | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| DISTRIBUTION_CENTER | Distribution center | A distribution center for the party |
| PROCESSING_FACILITY | Processing facility | A processing facility for the party |
| PROCESSING_PLANT | Processing plant | A processing plant for the party. |
